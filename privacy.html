CueSight Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

The short version: CueSight does all of its analysis on your device. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share your camera images, microphone audio, or any personal data. There are no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

What CueSight does

CueSight is an educational tool that reads facial expressions and behavioral cues from your device's camera and (optionally) microphone in real time, to help you learn about emotional expressions and conversational cues. It is a cue reader, not a lie detector, and no result it shows should be treated as proof of anything.

Camera and microphone

  • Camera: Used only to analyze facial expressions live, on your device. Video frames are processed in the moment and are never recorded, saved, or sent anywhere.
  • Microphone (optional): If you turn the mic on, audio is analyzed on-device for pitch and pause patterns only. Audio is never recorded, saved, or transmitted. The mic is off by default and you control it.

Data we store on your device

The only data CueSight saves is your baseline and calibration profile (numeric cue averages, not images or audio), kept in your browser's local storage on your device so you don't have to re-calibrate each session. You can erase it any time by clearing the app's site data. This information never leaves your device.

Data we collect

None. CueSight has no backend servers, no user accounts, no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking. We cannot see any of your data because it never reaches us.

Children

CueSight is not directed at children under 13 and collects no personal information from anyone.

Consent and responsible use

You are responsible for getting the consent of anyone you point the camera or microphone at, and for complying with the recording and privacy laws where you live. Many places require all parties to consent before audio is captured.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new date.
